Burmese Cuisine in Town

Two Wabbits is a home kitchen that serves home-cooked Burmese dishes and snacks, all made with love.

The name is in honour of the twin daughters of the founder, a devoted mom who is passionate about cooking, especially for her loved ones.

It is also a nod to the founder’s beloved younger sister.

We are a tiny, family-run outfit that really loves food and is always looking out for cooking up delicious meals. We are originally from Burma, so our expertise is Burmese cuisine – something we hope that would be enjoyed not just by our fellow Burmese people, but by everyone.

Thanks for visiting us. Hope you guys enjoy our tasty creations.
